How it’s made?

We select the tastiest milk from ecological places in our Ukraine. We carefully check all ingredients in our laboratory. Only after that, we mix them according to our excellent recipes and in strict compliance with state regulations, filter and perform pasteurization (heat treatment). The next stage on the way to this delicious mixture is the homogenizer - it creates a homogeneous mixture. After that, it goes to special tanks (freezers), which freeze and saturate the mixture with oxygen. And only after that, the finished ice cream is formed by dispensers, jam, chocolate or other goodies are added :) That's it, ready-made ice cream goes to the freezers of our warehouse, and from there to the counters of shops, cafes, restaurants or other wonderful places of our Ukraine! It remains for you to choose the flavor and enjoy this unique ice cream that we make for your pleasure!
